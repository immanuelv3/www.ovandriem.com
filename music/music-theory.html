        Explanation: 

        The "tempo-+characteristic-character" are indicating the character of the tempo
        and also the character of a rythm: each rythm has different rules like e.g. certain 
        intonations and tempi/(tempos) besides the tempo of the value of notes!... 
        E.g. a 3/4 rythm is counted on the 1st note and has a tempo close to the 
        value of a 4th note. 
        The 3/4 rythm and also 6/8 rythm are typical rythms for the 
        walz-music. You can search for examples of the different rythm-styles on e.g. 
        www.youtube.com. 

        "Indication of tempo-value of a note/the notes" stands for the value within 
        the rythm, the "tempo-+characteristic-character" as a whole determines it more 
        outside of it.
